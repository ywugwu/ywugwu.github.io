Yu Wu

Yu Wu (吴钰) is currently a Machine Learning Engineer at Meta. His research interests span a broad range of topics in Machine Learning. He is currently focused on scaling industrial machine learning systems with pretrained foundation models.

He received his Master’s degree in Computer Science from the Department of Computer Science at George Washington University, where he was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Robert Pless.

Before that, he studied Mathematics and Computer Science at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in Shenzhen, China.
